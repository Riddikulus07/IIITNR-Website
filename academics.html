I Love IIIT Naya Raipur | Academics


I love IIIT Naya Raipur for its innovative approach to academics. IIIT-NR offers cutting-edge education and a wide variety of programs, including B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. courses. Here are some key features:

  • Engineering courses from the first semester, not the 12th-grade curriculum.
  • Skill-first, theory-later model to build a solid foundation.
  • Fractal Academics with short modular courses to include the latest technologies.
  • 'T'-Education model to ensure both depth and breadth of knowledge.
  • Elective subjects and interdisciplinary projects.
  • Strong industry links for internships and industry-driven subjects.

COURSES OFFERED

  • B.TECH

    • IIIT-NR offers a range of B.Tech programs, including Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), and Data Science & Artificial Intelligence (DSAI). These dynamic programs prepare students for the ever-evolving tech landscape.

  • M.TECH

    • The M.Tech program at IIIT-NR offers specializations in Computer Science and Electronics and Communication Engineering. Students can delve into areas like Data Science, AI, Information Security, and more.

  • Ph.D.

    • IIIT-NR also offers Ph.D. programs in various disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics, Data Science, Physics, and more. The Ph.D. program emphasizes research and product development.
